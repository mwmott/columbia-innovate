Columbia J-School Innovate

In this project, Columbia Journalism School Students are looking into how to encourage more student-driven innovation at the school. We hope to build an innovation lab to empower journalism students to learn new technologies, build start-ups and better prepare to enter the changing world of journalism.

Project Goals

  • Short-term: A several-day walkthrough of the basics of start-ups, and a team participating in a Columbia Organization for Rising Entrepreneurs event.

  • Long-term: We hope to help develop infrastructure in the school that supports student innovation in spaces of journalism, technology and entrepreneurship. We've been looking at the Stabile program at the school, where students work in the same classes together all year, and also the The Documentary Project, where students could spend a third semester on an innovation or start-up.
